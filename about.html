About Us

Mission Diksha is an online tutoring platform that enables students to acquire knowledge with live classes by India's best faculties. With an aim to spread knowledge with culture, Dr. Sandeep Kishore Singh and Ms. Amita Singh started this company in the year 2020.

The idea of Mission Diksha was seeded when the world was plagued with pandemic. It derived with a mission to educate every child at home and make learning accessible, affordable and convenient to all.

Mission Diksha enables LIVE interactive learning between a teacher and a student. A platform adapted to give personalized teaching to every student using two-way audio, video and whiteboarding tools where both teacher and student are able to see, hear, write and interact in real-time.
